First, let me say that I love being an educator! As a teacher, I am passionate about guiding students in developing critical 21st century technology skills and the aptitudes needed for their future endeavors. It is paramount to help each individual student find ways to be successful, support them to grow academically and socially, and create an environment that promotes the most positive experience possible. This will be my 20th year teaching in the AUSD. It is a truly wonderful place. Great families, great kids, great colleagues and a great community! Let's have a great year and CODE ON!!!
